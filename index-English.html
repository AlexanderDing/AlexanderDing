ABOUT US

BDM studio, founded in Changsha in April 2021, has been deeply engaged in the field of design for three months. It has gathered 8 designers, photographers, illustrators, artists and creative people, and has a certain influence and appeal in the design creative group.
At the beginning of its establishment, BDM took "making design more valuable" as its mission and was committed to creating "BDM original copyright ecosystem" with original design as its core. It provides high-quality professional services for creative design practitioners in learning, exhibition, communication, employment, trading and entrepreneurship, and provides efficient copyright solutions and three-dimensional visual services for designers and enterprises.
